# Product Overview

Pets Almeida is a pet grooming and transportation service landing page for a business located in São Paulo, Brazil (Zona Norte region). The site serves as the primary digital presence for the business, focusing on lead generation and appointment scheduling via WhatsApp.

## Core Services

- Banho (bathing) and Tosa (grooming) for dogs and cats
- Táxi Dog (pet transportation service)
- Multiple grooming packages by pet size (Mini, Pequeno, Médio, Grande)
- Specialized services: Spitz grooming, scissor grooming, felino (cat) bathing

## Target Audience

Pet owners in São Paulo's Zona Norte neighborhoods, specifically:
- Cachoeirinha, Casa Verde, Limão, Vila Penteado, Brasilândia, Eliza Maria

## Business Goals

- Drive WhatsApp conversions for appointment scheduling
- Display service pricing transparently
- Build trust through professional presentation
- Provide neighborhood-specific landing pages for local SEO
